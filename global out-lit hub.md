# Global Outlet Hub -- Graduation Project (2025)

An online shopping application offering global brands at competitive
prices, designed for young users who value fashion, authenticity, and
seamless digital shopping experiences.

## Project Idea

Global Outlet Hub is a modern e-commerce experience that brings global
brands to young shoppers (ages 16--35) at more affordable prices. The
platform focuses on: - Fashion
- Original branded products
- Smooth digital shopping experience
- Affordable global items

## Target Audience

-   Users aged 16--35
-   Fashion-interested shoppers
-   Online shopping enthusiasts
-   Buyers looking for authentic global brands at better prices

## Competitors

-   Amazon
-   Jumia
-   Noon
